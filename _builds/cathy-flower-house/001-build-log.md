## Project Start

Started this delightful Rolife miniature kit on December 26, 2023. Cathy's Flower House is a charming flower shop scene that combines architectural elements with beautiful botanical details.

## Kit Contents

The Rolife kit arrived well-packaged with:
- Pre-cut wooden structural pieces
- Detailed paper flower elements
- LED lighting components
- Miniature furniture and accessories
- Clear step-by-step instructions
- All necessary assembly materials

The build began with assembling the main wooden structure. The pre-cut pieces fit together well, creating the shop's framework. The quality of the laser-cut components made alignment straightforward.

## Build Reflections

### What Worked Well
- Clear, well-organized instructions
- High-quality pre-cut components
- Beautiful flower elements
- Integrated lighting system
- Satisfying assembly process

### Challenges
- Working with delicate paper flowers required patience
- Some small pieces needed careful glue application
- Took time to get flower arrangements looking natural

### Skills Developed
- Working with miniature kit components
- Paper crafting at small scale
- Miniature LED lighting installation
- Patience with fine detail work

## Time Investment

**Total Build Time:** Approximately 15-20 hours over 3 weeks

**Breakdown:**
- Structure assembly: 4-5 hours
- Interior details: 5-6 hours
- Flower arrangements: 4-5 hours
- Lighting and finishing: 2-3 hours

## Final Thoughts

Cathy's Flower House was an enjoyable and relaxing build. The Rolife kit provided everything needed for a successful project, and the result is a charming display piece that brings a smile every time I see it lit up.

This kit would be perfect for anyone interested in trying miniature building - it's accessible for beginners while still offering satisfying detail work.
