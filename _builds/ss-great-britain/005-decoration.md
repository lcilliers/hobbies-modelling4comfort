Fortunately I remembered to paint the masts, bowsprit, deck structures and chimney before assembling the sails.  I also sanded the hull down, removing all the rough glue edges and filling in some unwanted cracks. It was given a proper undercoat and painted in black.  The keel was painted in red. The propeller was cut from tin and fitted in place. The port line was painted with gloss paint, which did not help when I tried to glue in the ports, cut from light cardboard. I finally got them all in place. It all ended with cutting out the base and fitting the brackets.
