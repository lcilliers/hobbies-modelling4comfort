## Navigate by Interest

**New to Scratch Building?** Start with kit projects like [Bookshop Memories]({{ '/builds/bookshop-memories/001-build-log/' | relative_url }}) or [Cathy's Flower House]({{ '/builds/cathy-flower-house/001-build-log/' | relative_url }}) to see simpler construction methods.

**Interested in Plant Making?** Explore the [English Country Garden]({{ '/builds/english-country-garden/001-planning-research/' | relative_url }}) series and [Plant Displays]({{ '/projects/plant-displays/' | relative_url }}) for extensive botanical techniques.

**Want Advanced Techniques?** Dive into [Traditional English Cottage]({{ '/builds/traditional-english-cottage/001-planning/' | relative_url }}) for complex wall texturing, multi-faceted roofs, and architectural problem-solving, or [Caravaning]({{ '/builds/caravaning/001-planning-commission/' | relative_url }}) for vehicle construction and figure sculpting.

**Maritime Modeling?** Check out the [SS Great Britain]({{ '/builds/ss-great-britain/001-planning-research/' | relative_url }}) build logs for ship construction and historical accuracy research.
