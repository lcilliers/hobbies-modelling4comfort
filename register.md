## Benefits of Participating

- **Share your builds**: Get your work featured in our gallery
- **Learn from others**: Engage in discussions about techniques
- **Build connections**: Connect with fellow modellers worldwide
- **Contribute**: Help improve the site and community

## Submitting Content

Want to share your work on Modelling4Comfort? Here's how:

### For Gallery Submissions

1. Prepare high-quality photos of your completed build
2. Write a brief description of the project
3. Include details like:
   - Kit manufacturer and scale
   - Paints and techniques used
   - Build time and challenges
4. Submit through GitHub or contact us directly

### For Video Content

1. Upload your video to YouTube or Vimeo
2. Share the link with us through comments or GitHub
3. Include a description and any relevant details

### For Blog Posts

If you'd like to write a guest post or tutorial:

1. Prepare your content in Markdown format
2. Include any images
3. Submit via GitHub pull request or contact us

## Community Guidelines

To keep our community welcoming and helpful:

- **Be respectful** of other builders and their work
- **Provide constructive feedback** when commenting
- **Share knowledge** freely with fellow hobbyists
- **Credit sources** when sharing techniques or ideas
- **Keep it on topic** - this is a modelling community

## Questions?

If you have any questions about participating, feel free to reach out through any of the channels above. We're always happy to help new members get started!
