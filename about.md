## About Modelling4Comfort

Modelling4Comfort is a hobby website dedicated to sharing the joy of model building with enthusiasts around the world. Our mission is to create a welcoming community where modellers of all skill levels can share their work, learn new techniques, and connect with like-minded hobbyists.

### What We Offer

- **Photo Gallery**: A curated collection of model builds showcasing various techniques and styles
- **Video Content**: Tutorials, build guides, and time-lapse videos of projects in progress
- **Blog**: Regular updates, tips, tricks, and features from our community
- **Community Interaction**: Comments and discussions on all content

### Our Focus Areas

- Scale models (aircraft, ships, vehicles, armor)
- Dioramas and vignettes
- Miniatures (wargaming, fantasy, sci-fi)
- Scratch-building and kit-bashing
- Painting and weathering techniques

### Contact

Have questions or want to contribute? Feel free to reach out through the comments on any post, or connect with us on [GitHub](https://github.com/lcilliers).
