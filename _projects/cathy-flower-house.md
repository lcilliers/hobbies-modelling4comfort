Cathy's Flower House is a charming miniature kit from Rolife featuring a detailed flower shop interior. This delightful kit combines architectural elements with botanical details, creating a cozy and inviting miniature world.

## Project Overview

**Build Type:** Kit Build (Rolife)  
**Scale:** 1:24  
**Status:** Completed  
**Start Date:** December 26, 2023  
**Completion:** January 2024

## Kit Details

**Manufacturer:** Rolife  
**Product Line:** DIY Miniature House  
**Pieces:** Multiple components including wooden parts, paper flowers, LED lighting, and accessories

### Features
- Pre-cut wooden pieces
- Detailed paper flower elements
- LED lighting system
- Furniture and decorative accessories
- Clear assembly instructions

## Build Highlights

This kit offered a wonderful introduction to miniature house building with:

- **Easy Assembly** - Pre-cut pieces made construction straightforward
- **Beautiful Details** - Intricate flower arrangements and shop decorations
- **Lighting Effects** - Integrated LED system brings the scene to life
- **Finished Appeal** - Makes an excellent display piece

## Learning Experience

Building Cathy's Flower House provided experience with:
- Following detailed assembly instructions
- Working with delicate paper elements
- Installing miniature LED lighting
- Creating realistic flower arrangements at small scale
- Patience with fine detail work

## Display

The completed flower house makes a charming display piece, perfect for a shelf or desk. The LED lighting creates a warm, inviting atmosphere that showcases the intricate details of the flower shop interior.
