Are you new to scale modelling? This guide will help you get started with the basics and set you on your way to creating amazing models.

## What You'll Need

### Essential Tools

1. **Hobby Knife** - For cutting parts from sprues and trimming
2. **Cutting Mat** - Protects your work surface
3. **Plastic Cement** - For joining plastic parts
4. **Sandpaper/Files** - For smoothing surfaces
5. **Tweezers** - For handling small parts

### Nice to Have

- Sprue cutters
- Pin vise with drill bits
- Putty for filling gaps
- Airbrush (for advanced painting)

## Choosing Your First Kit

For beginners, we recommend:

- **Snap-fit kits** - No glue required
- **1/72 or 1/48 scale aircraft** - Good detail without being too small
- **1/35 scale vehicles** - Larger parts, easier to handle
- **Starter sets** - Often include paint and basic tools

## Basic Assembly Steps

1. **Read the instructions** - Familiarize yourself with the build sequence
2. **Clean the parts** - Remove from sprues carefully
3. **Test fit** - Check how parts fit before gluing
4. **Apply glue sparingly** - Less is more
5. **Allow proper drying time** - Patience is key

## Tips for Success

> **Pro Tip**: Take your time! Rushing leads to mistakes. Enjoy the process.

- Work in a well-lit area
- Keep your workspace organized
- Practice on cheaper kits first
- Don't be afraid to make mistakes - that's how you learn!

## What's Next?

Once you've mastered basic assembly, explore:

- Painting techniques
- Weathering effects
- Decal application
- Display and photography
