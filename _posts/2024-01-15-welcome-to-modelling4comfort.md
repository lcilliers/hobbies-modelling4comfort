Welcome to Modelling4Comfort, your new home for all things model building!

We're excited to launch this community space dedicated to hobbyists who share a passion for creating miniature works of art. Whether you build scale aircraft, warships, vehicles, dioramas, or fantasy miniatures, this is the place to share, learn, and connect.

## What You'll Find Here

### Gallery
Our [gallery](/gallery/) showcases completed builds from our community. Submit your own work to be featured!

### Videos
Watch [tutorials and build guides](/videos/) to pick up new techniques and see how others approach their projects.

### Blog
Stay updated with news, tips, and features right here on the blog.

### Community
Use the comments section on any post or gallery item to join the conversation. We use Disqus for comments, so you can sign in with your existing social accounts.

## Getting Involved

We encourage everyone to participate! Here are some ways to get started:

1. **Browse the content** - Explore the gallery and blog posts
2. **Leave comments** - Share your thoughts and feedback
3. **Submit your work** - Got a build you're proud of? We'd love to feature it
4. **Share techniques** - Write a guest post or contribute a tutorial

## What's Next

We're just getting started! In the coming weeks, we'll be:

- Adding more gallery content
- Publishing technique tutorials
- Featuring community builds
- Expanding our video library

Thank you for being part of our community from the beginning. We can't wait to see what you create!
